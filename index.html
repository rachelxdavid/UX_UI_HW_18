Rachel Was Here

I'm a UX/UI designer who holds the belief that every pixel can serve as a vessel for empathy, and I thrive on the challenge of infusing each project with a deep sense of narrative that reflects the richness of the human experience.

My Work

The Center website redesign

The Center on Colfax provides resources to engage, empower, enrich, and advance the queer community. We wanted to redesign the website to create a more intuitive user flow with design elements that highlighted the diverse, colorful personalities within the community they represent.

Sustenance

Sustenance was designed to simplify the meal planning process by providing a way for the user to keep track of the ingredients they already have and offer a variety of user-centric recipes. The hope is to see less food waste and less time spent meal planning.

Zeal

Zeal is an activity planning app that helps the adventurous traveler customize their trip by providing a way to discover unique experiences that are best suited for their personal interests.

My Skills

User Research

I employ a diverse range of research methods, from the creation of empathy maps to user interviews, with the goal of unearthing valuable insights that drive the design process. Ensuring that the end product truly addresses the user's needs and pain points.

User Empathy

I actively seek to understand and connect with users on a personal level, allowing their experiences and emotions to inform every design decision I make, ultimately creating interfaces that resonate deeply and deliver a seamless user journey.

Qualitative Interviews

When interviewing, I create a space that encourage users to share their authentic thoughts, emotions, and experiences. This allows me to gain insights into their needs and preferences, which in turn, informs design decisions that prioritize their satisfaction and usability.
